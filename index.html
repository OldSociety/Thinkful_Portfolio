Who is Austin Lance?

Why I Do This?

I have a passion for creating worlds and bringing potential experiences, especially those with a cultural impact, to life. I enjoy working with other creatives and combining our skills to make something greater than what we could possibly do ourselves.

When I am not developing, I stay busy writing fiction, traveling, going out dancing on the weekends, playing popular video games, and hosting weekly board game nights with my friends.
